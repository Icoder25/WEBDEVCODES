Frequently Asked Questions

How fast is ZenoCare?

ZenoCare dispatches ambulances within 2-3 minutes of booking. Our AI-powered system ensures the fastest possible response time by pre-positioning ambulances based on demand patterns.

Is it free?

ZenoCare booking is free. You only pay standard ambulance charges directly to the service provider. No hidden fees, no surge pricing during emergencies.

Can I use it for my parents?

Absolutely! You can book ambulances for family members and add their profiles to your account. Emergency contacts are automatically notified when you book for others.

Do hospitals know I'm coming?

Yes! ZenoCare automatically informs the destination hospital about incoming patients, ensuring beds are reserved and medical teams are prepared for your arrival.
