Orders & Shipping

The minimum order quantity varies by product and is clearly indicated on each product page. Typically, our MOQs range from 12 to 50 units depending on the product category and price point. Bulk orders qualify for tiered pricing discounts.

We offer two shipping options:

  • Standard Shipping: 3-5 business days for most locations in India
  • Express Shipping: 1-2 business days for urgent orders

Shipping times may vary based on your location and product availability. You can track your order status through your account dashboard once the order is dispatched.

Shipping costs are calculated based on order weight, delivery location, and shipping method:

  • Standard Shipping: ₹150-₹300 depending on weight and location
  • Express Shipping: ₹300-₹600 depending on weight and location
  • Free Standard Shipping: Available on orders above ₹10,000

Exact shipping costs will be calculated at checkout based on your cart contents and delivery address.

Once your order is dispatched, you will receive a tracking number via email and SMS. You can track your order in two ways:

  1. Log in to your account and visit the "Order History" section
  2. Use the tracking number on our shipping partner's website

If you have any issues tracking your order, please contact our customer support team.

Yes, we ship to all major cities and most locations across India. However, delivery times may vary for remote areas. We currently do not offer international shipping.

If you're in a remote location, please contact our customer service team before placing an order to confirm delivery availability and estimated timeframes.

Products & Pricing

Our tiered pricing structure offers volume-based discounts. The more units you purchase, the lower the price per unit. For example, a product might be priced as follows:

  • 12-49 units: ₹140/unit
  • 50-99 units: ₹125/unit
  • 100+ units: ₹110/unit

The applicable tier is automatically calculated in your cart based on the quantity you select. Each product has its own specific tier structure displayed on the product detail page.

We maintain strict quality control processes and can provide certification documentation upon request for bulk orders.

Payment & Credit

We accept a variety of payment methods to accommodate different business needs:

  • Online Payments: Credit/Debit Cards, Net Banking, UPI
  • Bank Transfer: NEFT/RTGS/IMPS
  • Digital Wallets: PayTM, Google Pay, PhonePe
  • Credit Terms: 30% advance payment with approved credit application

All online transactions are secured with industry-standard encryption to protect your payment information.

Our credit payment system is designed to help businesses manage cash flow while building inventory:

  1. Application: Complete our credit application form with business details and trade references
  2. Approval: Credit applications are typically processed within 3-5 business days
  3. Terms: Approved businesses can place orders with 30% advance payment
  4. Balance Payment: The remaining 70% is due within 30 days of invoice date
  5. Credit Limit: Initial credit limits are based on business history and references

Credit terms are available for registered businesses with a minimum of 1 year in operation and valid GSTIN. Credit limits can be increased over time with a good payment history.

We provide proper GST invoices for all transactions, which you can download from your account dashboard or receive via email.

Returns & Refunds

We accept returns under the following conditions:

  • Damaged or Defective Products: Full refund or replacement within 7 days of delivery
  • Incorrect Items: Full refund or replacement if we shipped the wrong product
  • Unopened Products: Returns accepted within 3 days of delivery with 10% restocking fee

To initiate a return, please contact our customer service team with your order number and details of the issue. We'll provide a return authorization and instructions for shipping the items back to us.

Please note that custom orders, private label products, and clearance items are not eligible for returns unless damaged or defective.

If you receive damaged or defective products, please follow these steps:

  1. Inspect all products immediately upon delivery
  2. Take clear photographs of the damaged/defective items and packaging
  3. Contact our customer service team within 48 hours of delivery
  4. Provide your order number, product details, and photographs
  5. Our team will review your claim and provide resolution options

For damaged shipments, we recommend noting the damage on the delivery receipt before accepting the package. This helps expedite the claims process with our shipping partners.

Our refund process typically follows this timeline:

  1. Return Approval: 1-2 business days after receiving your request
  2. Return Shipping: Depends on your location (typically 2-5 days)
  3. Quality Check: 1-2 business days after we receive the returned items
  4. Refund Processing: 3-5 business days after quality check approval
  5. Bank/Card Processing: 2-7 business days (varies by payment method)

The total refund process usually takes 7-14 business days from the date we receive your returned items. For credit orders, refunds are processed as credits to your account balance or adjusted against future invoices.

Our return shipping policy depends on the reason for the return:

  • Our Error (wrong item, damaged in transit): We cover return shipping costs
  • Product Defect: We cover return shipping costs
  • Customer Change of Mind: Customer is responsible for return shipping costs
  • Bulk Returns (over ₹10,000): Case-by-case evaluation

When we cover shipping costs, we'll either arrange pickup through our shipping partners or reimburse your shipping expenses (with prior approval and valid receipt).

Yes, we offer product exchanges as an alternative to refunds. The exchange process works as follows:

  1. Contact our customer service team to request an exchange
  2. Specify which products you want to return and what you'd like instead
  3. We'll verify product availability and price differences
  4. Return your original items as instructed
  5. Once we receive and inspect the returns, we'll ship your exchange items

If there's a price difference between the returned and exchanged items, we'll either charge or refund the difference. Exchanges are subject to the same eligibility criteria as our return policy.
