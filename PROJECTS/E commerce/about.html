Our Story

Shubham Enterprise was founded in 2010 by Rajesh Sharma, a visionary entrepreneur with a passion for the beauty industry. What began as a small distribution business in Delhi has now grown into one of North India's leading wholesale suppliers of premium cosmetics and beauty products.

Our journey started with a simple mission: to provide high-quality, affordable cosmetics to salons and retailers across India. Over the years, we've expanded our product range, strengthened our supply chain, and built lasting relationships with our customers.

Today, Shubham Enterprise serves over 500 businesses nationwide, from independent salons to large retail chains. We take pride in our commitment to quality, competitive pricing, and exceptional customer service.
