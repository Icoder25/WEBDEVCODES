Shipping Policy

Information about our shipping methods, delivery times, and costs

Shipping Information

At Shubham Enterprise, we are committed to delivering your wholesale cosmetics and beauty products safely and efficiently. This shipping policy outlines our shipping methods, delivery timeframes, costs, and other important information to ensure a smooth delivery experience.

Shipping Methods

We offer the following shipping methods for all orders within India:

Shipping Method Estimated Delivery Time Cost
Standard Shipping 3-5 business days ₹150-₹300 (based on weight and location)
Express Shipping 1-2 business days ₹300-₹600 (based on weight and location)
Free Standard Shipping 3-5 business days Free on orders above ₹10,000

Please note that business days are Monday through Friday, excluding national holidays. Orders placed after 2:00 PM IST may be processed the following business day.

Shipping Coverage

We currently ship to all major cities and most locations across India. Delivery to remote areas may take additional time. We do not currently offer international shipping.

Major Cities with Express Shipping

Delhi NCR, Mumbai, Bangalore, Chennai, Hyderabad, Kolkata, Pune, Ahmedabad, Jaipur, Lucknow, Chandigarh, Bhopal, Indore, Nagpur, Kochi, Coimbatore, Visakhapatnam

For locations not listed above, please contact our customer service team to confirm shipping availability and estimated delivery times before placing your order.

Order Processing

Our order processing timeline is as follows:

  1. Order Verification: 1-2 hours after order placement
  2. Payment Confirmation: Immediate for online payments; 1-2 business days for bank transfers
  3. Order Processing: 1-2 business days (packaging and preparation)
  4. Dispatch: Orders are typically dispatched within 24-48 hours after processing

Important Note

During peak seasons (festivals, sales events), order processing may take an additional 1-2 business days. We appreciate your patience during these busy periods.

Shipping Costs

Shipping costs are calculated based on the following factors:

  • Order Weight: Heavier packages incur higher shipping costs
  • Delivery Location: Distance from our warehouse affects shipping costs
  • Shipping Method: Express shipping costs more than standard shipping
  • Order Value: Orders above ₹10,000 qualify for free standard shipping

The exact shipping cost will be calculated and displayed at checkout before you complete your purchase. All shipping costs are inclusive of applicable taxes.

Order Tracking

Once your order is dispatched, you will receive a tracking number via email and SMS. You can track your order in the following ways:

  1. Log in to your account on our website and visit the "Order History" section
  2. Click on the tracking link provided in the dispatch confirmation email
  3. Use the tracking number on our shipping partner's website
  4. Contact our customer service team with your order number

If you don't receive a tracking number within 48 hours of receiving your order confirmation (for in-stock items), please contact our customer service team.

Shipping Delays

While we strive to deliver all orders within the estimated timeframes, certain factors beyond our control may cause delays:

  • Weather Conditions: Severe weather may impact transportation networks
  • Natural Disasters: Floods, earthquakes, or other natural events
  • Transportation Strikes: Labor disputes affecting shipping carriers
  • Public Holidays: National or regional holidays may delay processing or delivery
  • Incorrect Address Information: Please ensure your shipping address is accurate

In case of any significant delays, our customer service team will notify you via email or phone and provide updated delivery estimates.

Shipping Restrictions

Please be aware of the following shipping restrictions:

  • Hazardous Materials: Certain products containing alcohol or aerosols may have shipping restrictions
  • Remote Locations: Some extremely remote areas may not be serviceable or may require additional delivery time
  • PO Boxes: We do not ship to PO Box addresses
  • International: We currently do not ship outside of India

If you have concerns about shipping restrictions for your location or specific products, please contact our customer service team before placing your order.

Receiving Your Order

When receiving your order, please follow these guidelines:

  1. Inspect the Package: Check for any visible damage before accepting delivery
  2. Count the Items: Verify that all ordered items are included in the shipment
  3. Check Product Condition: Ensure products are in good condition and properly sealed
  4. Report Issues: Report any discrepancies or damages within 48 hours of delivery

Tip for Business Customers

For large orders, we recommend having staff available to receive and inspect the shipment. If possible, schedule delivery during your regular business hours to ensure proper receipt and inspection.

Frequently Asked Shipping Questions

Once your order is dispatched, you will receive a tracking number via email and SMS. You can track your order by logging into your account and visiting the "Order History" section, or by using the tracking link provided in the dispatch confirmation email.

We currently do not offer same-day delivery. Our fastest shipping option is Express Shipping, which delivers within 1-2 business days for most major cities. For urgent orders, please contact our customer service team to discuss expedited shipping options.

If you're not available to receive your delivery, our shipping partners will typically make 2-3 delivery attempts. After unsuccessful attempts, the package may be held at the local delivery office for pickup, or returned to our warehouse. You'll receive notifications about delivery attempts and next steps via SMS and email.

Address changes can only be accommodated if the order has not yet been processed for shipping. Please contact our customer service team immediately if you need to change your shipping address. Once an order has been dispatched, we cannot change the delivery address.
